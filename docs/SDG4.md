# SDG 4: Quality Education

**Goal:** Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

### Intersection with this Project
Education is the cornerstone of sustainable development. By using AI to predict student performance, we addresses several key challenges:

1.  **Early Identification**: Detecting students who are "Needs Improvement" early in the semester allows for personalized tutoring.
2.  **Resource Allocation**: Schools can allocate mentors more efficiently.
3.  **Reducing Attrition**: Predictive models help in reducing dropout rates by flagging disengagement (low attendance) early.
4.  **Data-Driven Insights**: Helps administrators understand which factors (e.g., Attendance vs. Study Hours) have the most significant impact on student success.

By automating this analysis, we make high-quality educational monitoring accessible to institutions regardless of their size.
